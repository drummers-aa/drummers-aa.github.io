The Drummers Group of AA

Drummers is an LGBTQ-friendly Alcoholics Anonymous group, but in accordance with AA traditions, all are welcome!

Drummers meets every day at 8pm, except Sunday which is at 1:30pm. See the meeting list.

Drummers meets at the Short North Church in Columbus Ohio's Short North neighborhood:

Short North Church
25 W 5th St
Columbus OH 43201

Potluck Dinner is back

Potluck is back on the last Friday of every month.

The last Friday of every month is the Anniversary meeting where we celebrate all anniversaries for the month. Starting in April the potluck dinner is back.

Bring in a dish and share food and fellowship, starting at 7:30. The anniversary meeting starts at 8pm.
